# AdventureWorks: Data-Driven Strategies for Competitive Advantage in the Cycling Industry

# *Project Overview

This project focuses on solving real-world business challenges faced by AdventureWorks, a global cycling company. Despite steady revenue growth, 
the company struggles with fluctuating sales, increasing product returns, regional performance gaps, and declining profit margins.

Using Power BI, this project applies data-driven analytics to uncover actionable insights that can improve decision-making, reduce returns,
and enhance overall business performance.

# Business Objectives

 * Analyze total revenue, profit, and sales trends
 * Identify high-performing products and regions
 * Evaluate return rates and their impact on profitability
 * Understand customer demographics and behavior
 * Support strategic decision-making using data insights


# Key Business Challenges

*   Fluctuating quarterly sales trends (seasonality impact)
*   Increasing product returns (especially in accessories)
*  Regional disparities in sales performance
*  Customer churn in younger demographics
*  Economic pressures reducing profit margins



# Solution Approach (Power BI)

The project leverages Power BI to:

* Analyze historical sales trends
* Identify high-return products
* Optimize regional sales strategies
* Segment customers based on demographics
* Evaluate profitability and return impact
* Enable data-driven decision-making


# Key Metrics (KPIs)

* Total Revenue
* Total Profit
* Profit Margin (%)
* Return Rate (%)
* Net Profit (after returns)
* Total Customers
* Average Annual Income


# Key Insights

* Majority of revenue is generated from top-performing regions
* Product returns negatively impact overall profitability
* Sales show strong seasonal trends, especially in Q4
* Customer segmentation helps identify high-value groups
* Regional performance highlights untapped market opportunities


# Business Questions Solved

1. Total revenue generated across all years
2. Highest-selling product category
3. Profit margin by region
4. Total customers and average income
5. Monthly and yearly sales trends
6. Year-over-Year (YoY) revenue growth
7. Return rate by product subcategory
8. Net profit after accounting for returns
9. Sales performance by region/country
10. Customer segmentation (income & marital status)
11. Top 10 products by profit
12. Impact of customer age on purchasing behavior
13. Sales forecasting (time series)
14. Impact of discounts on profit
15. Cohort analysis by customer behavior
